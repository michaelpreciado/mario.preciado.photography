About Me

As an Oakland based photographer, I specialize in capturing authentic portraits and lifestyle moments. With 8 years behind the lens documenting countless stories and milestones, I've developed an eye for those genuine, unscripted moments that make each shoot unique.

I love working with natural light and finding creative angles to craft images that feel both modern and timeless. My goal is to make you feel comfortable and confident, allowing your true personality to shine through in every frame.

I'm available for portrait sessions, special events, brand collaborations and commercial work. Let's create something amazing together.
