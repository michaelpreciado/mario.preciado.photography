ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Mario Preciado, a professional photographer based in Oakland, California. I specialize in portrait and lifestyle photography, capturing authentic moments that tell your unique story.

With over 10 years of experience, I've developed a distinctive style that blends artistic vision with technical precision. My approach is collaborative and relaxed, ensuring that you feel comfortable in front of the camera.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring the Bay Area's vibrant art scene, hiking in the beautiful California landscapes, or experimenting with new photography techniques.

I'm available for portrait sessions, events, and commercial projects. Let's create something beautiful together!
